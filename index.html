My journey

My journey with art started so many years ago. As a kid, I loved colors and all the beautiful colored images on my schoolbooks and storybooks. I loved it so much that I decided to start recreating cartoon characters and abstract images that I had seen. Like Tweety in the Sylvester and Tweety cartoon or the characters in Archie comics. I enjoyed drawing and painting with colorful markers. As I got a bit older, I extended my love for drawing and painting into making crafts like Christmas and birthday cards, beaded accessories, crochets, etc. I even had so many scrapbooks collection of different things that I had drawn.
